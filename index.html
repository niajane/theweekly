The Weekly

Hear from the people you care about.

What is it?

  • A weekly newsletter filled with sections from your friends.
  • Currently just text, up to 300 words. 20 words equally welcome!
  • Write whatever you feel like. Something you felt, something you read, something you made, something you experienced, something you thought. Perhaps a request - looking for book recommendations? Feedback on something?
  • Stuck? Use the weekly prompt if you want.
  • Want to comment on what someone wrote? Call or text them xoxo

How does it work?

It's all email-based.
  • To join, email join@theweekly.ink with your name in the subject
  • To add a friend, email friend@theweekly.ink with their email address.
  • To submit for the week, reply to last week's newsletter. To re-submit, reply again. The latest submission will be added.
  • The newsletter will be sent out at 9 AM BST every Sunday(or should it be 9am the place where it is that time first?)
  • This is an MMVP (mega minimum viable product) so it may not work. In progress!
  • Anything else, things I have not thought of yet (changing your name? unfriending?), feedback, requests, email help@theweekly.ink
